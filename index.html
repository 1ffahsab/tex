About Us

Our Mission

To provide innovative e-waste recycling solutions that protect the environment and promote sustainability through responsible disposal of electronic devices.

Our Vision

A world where every electronic device is responsibly recycled, minimizing environmental impact and maximizing resource recovery for future generations.

Our Values

Commitment to environmental stewardship, transparency in our processes, and community engagement in creating sustainable practices.

Recycling Guide

1

Identify Your E-Waste

Collect your old electronics: computers, printers, phones, batteries, and accessories. Sort them by type for proper processing.

Computers Phones Batteries Accessories
2

Prepare Items

Backup and erase personal data from devices. Remove batteries from electronics when possible. Keep cables and accessories together.

Backup personal data
Perform factory reset
Remove batteries
3

Find a Center

Use our center locator to find certified e-waste recycling facilities near you. Check their accepted items and operating hours.

Browse Centers
4

Drop Off

Bring your items to the selected recycling center during operating hours. Some centers also offer pick-up services for bulk items.
